Reimagine Reimbursements

When employees make transactions using the Reimburse Network, their business expenses are added to a smart contract enabling employers to seamlessly process reimbursements

Simple & Efficient

Reducing Friction in Reimbursements

Employee

Make business related purchases at vendors around the world that are automatically logged on the blockchain. No more tedious expense reports!

Blockchain

Every smart contract on the blockchain includes all the necessary information for an organization to log expenses. No more manual coding of expenses!

Employers

Expenditures are attached to the ledger immediately and reimbursement amounts are reconciled. No need to gather and input expense reports!

  • 1
    Employee Purchases

    Makes business related purchases and uses the ReimburseApp to send payment to vendors. Can make direct payments to ERC20 wallets, make atomic swaps with other cryptocurrencies, or integrate with traditional fiat payment gateways.

  • 2
    Smart Contracts

    Every transaction logged on the blockchain includes the expenditure category, amount of REIM spent, and USD/BTC equivalent. As soon as an expenditure occurs it fits neatly into the organization's ledger.

  • 3
    Employers

    As soon as an employee makes a business related transaction it is added to the organization's smart contract. Can reimburse employees with Reimburse Token, other cryptocurrencies via atomic swaps, or fiat with traditional pay systems.

Token Usage

The ReimburseToken (REIM) is necessary for Employees and Organizations to use the Reimburse transact in the ecosystem. It is the hub by which Employees make transactions and by which Organization track expenditures. As ecosystem adoption grows the tokens increase in equivalent USD value, and fractional tokens can be used for smaller reimbursements.
